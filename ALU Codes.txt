Codes to Implement in the ALU

==Combinational Logic Codes==

-Arithmatic-

1 Addition
1 Addition with Carry
1 Increment
2 Subtraction
2 Subtraction with Borrow
2 Decrement

-Logical-

NOT
AND
OR
XOR
Bit Shift Rotate Left
Bit Shift Rotate Right
Bit Shift Rotate through Carry Left
Bit Shift Rotate through Carry Right
Bit Shift Logical Left
Bit Shift Logical Right
Bit Shift Arithmatic Right

==Sequential Logic Codes==

-Arithmatic-

Multiplication
Division

-Logical-

NAND
NOR
XNOR



Note: The numbers before some of the operations indicate that the group of operations uses the same space architectually.
